# CMA Narrative

**Coming with Episode 6 — [CMA Narratives in 60 Seconds](https://youtube.com/@theprosperityagents)**

The full SKILL.md content drops the day this episode airs. Preview of what it'll do:

- Take 3-6 comps + the subject property data → produce a 4-section CMA narrative
- Comp Summary (1 paragraph)
- Price Positioning (1 paragraph — where this listing slots vs comps, why)
- Market Context (1 paragraph — town-level data, no scarcity language)
- Recommended List-Price Range (1 paragraph — high / low / strategic recommendation)

References [`fair-housing-overlay`](../fair-housing-overlay/) for compliance. Output is the WRITE-UP only — pair with [`canva/cma-cover`](../canva/cma-cover/) for the visual deck.

# MLS to Social

**Coming with Episode 8 — [Today's MLS Pull → 5 Social Posts](https://youtube.com/@theprosperityagents)**

The full SKILL.md drops the day this episode airs. Preview:

- Input: today's MLS new-listings export (paste or CSV path)
- Output: 5 platform posts
  - Instagram caption (≤2200 chars, 5 hashtags, no link)
  - Facebook post (≤700 chars, conversational, ends with question)
  - TikTok caption (≤300 chars, 5 hashtags)
  - X post (≤280 chars, 2 hashtags max)
  - LinkedIn post (300-500 words, professional tone, ends with insight)

Each post pulls 3-5 listings from the export and frames them positively. FH rules enforced: no schools, no demographics, no scarcity, always positive. Pair with [`canva/just-listed`](../canva/just-listed/) when you have a single hero listing.

References [`fair-housing-overlay`](../fair-housing-overlay/).

Subscribe to [@theprosperityagents](https://youtube.com/@theprosperityagents) for the drop.
